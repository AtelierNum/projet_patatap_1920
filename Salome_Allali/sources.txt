
For my patatap I wanted to start on a rather dark graphic line that reminds us of the night world to get closer to the style of electro music whether in color (blue and green) or in sounds that are short, so we can create music with the keyboard. My sounds also sound pretty electronic.  I was inspired by Martin Garrix's performance at the Vielles Charrues 2019
